Getting Started

What is Sapiens Tutor?

Sapiens Tutor is an AI-powered learning platform that provides personalized tutoring across various subjects. Our system adapts to your learning style and academic level to deliver the most effective educational experience.

How do I get started?

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Select your grade level and curriculum
  3. Choose a subject to study
  4. Start asking questions and learning!

Using the Tutor

How do I ask questions?

Simply select a subject, choose a topic, and type your question in the chat box. Our AI tutor will provide detailed explanations, step-by-step solutions, and additional examples to help you understand the concept.

What subjects are available?

  • Mathematics (Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, etc.)
  • Physics (Mechanics, Thermodynamics, etc.)
  • Chemistry (Organic, Inorganic, etc.)
  • Computer Science (Programming, Data Structures, etc.)

Account Settings

How do I change my grade level?

Go to your profile menu, select "Grade Level," and choose your current academic level. This helps us provide content that matches your educational needs.

How do I update my curriculum?

Access the profile menu, click on "Curriculum," and select from various options including General, IB, AP, Cambridge, and more.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Slow response times: Refresh the page or check your internet connection
  • Session errors: Try logging out and back in
  • Display issues: Clear your browser cache and cookies
